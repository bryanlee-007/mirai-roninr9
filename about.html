The People

Who We Are.

MIRAI Labo is a two-person operation. One drives the creative. One holds the architecture together. Neither role is decorative.

01
Creative Director, MIRAI Labo by Ronin XP

Bryan Lee

李實胤 · Kuala Lumpur

For twenty years Bryan Lee has worked as a strategic production partner to 4As agencies across Malaysia — delivering commercial work for Mercedes-Benz, Toyota, Adidas, Porsche, Ford, Castrol, AirAsia, Mitsubishi Motors, and Kao Malaysia. He approaches every brief as a designer first — problem before medium, solution before format. That discipline carried him from graphic and digital work into events and experiential production, then into virtual production, and now into synthesized reality.

Bryan founded MIRAI Labo as Malaysia’s first Synthesized Reality studio. Every project produced by the studio is directed under the Ghost in the Machine philosophy — the conviction that AI has no taste, no intent, no soul, and that the director’s job is to provide all three. He developed the Shinjitsu Standard as MIRAI Labo’s content provenance framework, integrating C2PA metadata and QR verification into every final deliverable. MIRAI Labo operates under the production infrastructure of Ronin XP.
